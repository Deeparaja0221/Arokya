Our History

Arokya, the flagship milk brand of Hatsun Agro Product Ltd, was launched in 1995 with a strong focus on purity, freshness, and quality. Starting with milk and curd, the brand quickly became a household name across South India by sourcing milk directly from lakhs of farmers in villages and ensuring it is pasteurized and homogenized under strict hygiene standards. In 2021, Arokya expanded its product line with the launch of paneer made through traditional methods. Today, Arokya stands as one of the most trusted dairy brands, offering a range of products like milk, curd, and paneer, loved by millions of families.
